Books in Progress

Covers and short synopses for books I'm developing. These are early concepts — each entry includes the idea and narrative focus.

Hollow Harbor

A coastal mystery about a small town holding onto secrets after a storm uncovers a forgotten shipwreck. Themes: memory, community, and the cost of silence.

Paper Cities

An interlinked short fiction collection exploring how maps—literal and emotional—reshape the lives of strangers who cross paths in unexpected ways.

Bright Archive

A nonfiction project tracing the history of small civic technologies and the people who built them, focusing on craft, failure, and persistence.
